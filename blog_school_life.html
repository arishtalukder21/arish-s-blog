My school life

I have just started 7th grade and everything is going well right now. Unlike last year, this year, we have been given lockers so it makes things more easier as we can now store our belongings there instead of having to carry it home everyday. ALso, unlike last year, this year we have to learn Spanish and there is a whole new subject for that. Right now, the normal classes have Spanish 2 times a week and the SP (honors classes) have Spanish 3 times a week, moving on to 8th grade, the normal classes will have Spanish 3 times a week and the SP classes will have Spanish 4 times a week and the SP class will also get a Spanish state test (a state test is basically like a state-wide test that determines how good you are and highschools also check this but it doesn't count toward your school grade). I'm currently enjoying 7th grade, I've made a few friends and I sometimes go out with them to play or just relax at the park or at my house or their house.
