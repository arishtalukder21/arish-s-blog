Where I'm from

Hi! My name is Arish. I was born in Dhaka, Bangladesh in April of 2012 and now I live in the United States. Being raised in Bangladesh, I know a few things about Bangladesh that might interest you.

  • Bangladesh has 6 seasons!
  • It's home to one of the longest beaches
  • Bangladesh has the world's largest mangrove forest
  • It is very hot and humid in Bangladesh throughout most of the year
  • Bangladesh is one of the world's largest garment exporter
  • Bangladesh's shopping mall (Jamuna Feature Park) is the largest shopping mall in all of South Asia!
