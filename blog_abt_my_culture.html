My culture and ethnicity

Being a Muslim from Bangladesh, my culture is a mix of Hinduism, Buddhism, and Islam with Islam having a bigger impact. Bangladesh is a melting pot of races, it has a very mixed and diverse culture. There is a strong tradition of music, dance, literature, drama, and arts. Some of the festivals in Bangladesh (unordered) are:

  • Pohela Boishakh (The first day of the Bengali Year)
  • Independece Day (26th March)
  • Victory Day(16th December)
  • Eid-ul Fitr
  • Eid-ul Adha
